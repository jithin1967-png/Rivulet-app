🔒 Privacy Policy

Pentawave Foundation collects personal data including name, phone number, email, and location to provide RIVULET platform services. All data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3).

We do not sell personal data to third parties. Data may be shared with partner stores and delivery agents solely to fulfil orders. Users may request data deletion at any time by contacting [email protected].

Platform fees are non-refundable unless a service failure is confirmed by Pentawave. Disputes are governed by the laws of Kerala, with jurisdiction in Kozhikode courts.

🌾 Seller Policy
›

All sellers must hold a valid FSSAI registration and provide proof of business (GST certificate or Udyam registration) before listing products on RIVULET.

Sellers are permitted a maximum of 3 simultaneous shelf space rentals across partner stores. Space rental is weekly, with a 48-hour cancellation notice required for refunds.

Product listings must include accurate descriptions, MRP, weight, and expiry date. Misleading listings will result in immediate delisting and potential account suspension.

Platform commission is 0.5% of each sale value, settled daily via auto-transfer to the seller's registered bank account.

🚚 Delivery Partner Policy
›

Delivery partners must possess a valid driving licence, vehicle registration, and third-party vehicle insurance before onboarding. Background verification is conducted by our operations team.

Delivery SLA is 90 minutes from order pickup. Partners who consistently miss SLA (more than 3 times in a week) may be temporarily suspended pending review.

Partners pay a ₹499/month subscription for platform access, which includes third-party liability insurance coverage for delivery-related incidents.

Earnings are calculated per delivery (₹80–₹200 based on distance and time slot) plus performance bonuses for top-rated partners.

🔄 Refund & Cancellation Policy
›

Customers may cancel orders within 30 minutes of placement for a full refund. Cancellations after 30 minutes are not eligible for refunds unless the order is undelivered.

If a wrong item is delivered, customers must report it within 24 hours via the app. Refunds are processed within 2–3 business days after admin verification.

Failed deliveries (second attempt also failed) are fully refunded within 24 hours. Space booking refunds are not available within 48 hours of the rental start date.

📊 Brand Analytics Policy
›

Brand Analytics PRO subscribers access aggregated, store-level sales data for their own SKUs only. No competitor SKU data is exposed without explicit consent from all parties.

AI-generated demand forecasts (via Jiya Jarvis) are advisory in nature. Pentawave makes no guarantee of forecast accuracy and is not liable for commercial decisions made based on AI insights.

Brand data is isolated per subscription — one brand cannot access another brand's analytics. Annual subscriptions are non-refundable after the first 14-day trial period.
